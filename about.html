About Us

Since 1971, Powering Progress with Precision.

Prakash Electronics was founded by Prakash Shah in 1971 with a simple mission — to bring world-class industrial electronic solutions to India’s growing manufacturing landscape.
What started as a small, family-run operation has grown into a trusted name in the import and export of high-quality heating elements and specialized industrial electronics.

Today, Prakash Electronics is led by the second generation — Nihir Shah and Snehal Shah, who have continued the legacy with the same values: integrity, innovation, and long-term partnerships.
With over five decades of experience, the company has built a reputation for delivering reliable, customized, and efficient solutions for clients across industries, both locally and globally.
From sourcing top-tier components to offering tailored advice and timely delivery, we don’t just supply — we solve problems, spark growth, and support your operations from the inside out.

This isn’t just business. It’s two generations of trust, commitment, and excellence.
